# ASSETS.md

This repository contains original **submod code** designed for use alongside **Empire at War Expanded (EaWX)** mods.

## Scope of the MIT License

The MIT License included in this repository applies **only** to original code authored by this project's contributors.

It **does not** apply to:
* EaWX base mod content.
* Art assets (models, textures, audio, UI elements).
* Any other content created by the EaWX team or its contributors.

## Dependency on EaWX

This submod depends on content provided by EaWX and is not intended to function standalone.
It includes only the files necessary to operate when used with the EaWX base mod.

Please see the `faq-and-info` channel on their official Discord for their usage policies.

Discord server invite: [Corey / Empire at War Expanded](https://discord.com/invite/coreyloses)

### EaWX submod policy

Per the EaWX team's published policies (summary):

* Community submods are allowed and encouraged.
* Submods may be released without requesting prior permission.
* Submods must not be full or near-full reuploads of the base mod.
* A reasonable effort should be made to include only necessary files.
* Submods should clearly state they are not affiliated with the EaWX team.
* EaWX content may be rearranged or referenced *within the EaWX ecosystem*.

### Use of EaWX content outside EaWX

Use of EaWX assets **outside the EaWX ecosystem** (in non-EaWX mods or standalone projects) is **not automatically permitted**.

If you wish to reuse EaWX assets outside of EaWX, please contact the EaWX team directly and follow their guidance.
